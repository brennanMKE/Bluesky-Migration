## Description

Manual sign-off for the Notifications tab — list rendering, reason
labelling, grouping, tap navigation, and the unread badge lifecycle.

## Test sequence

1. **Trigger notifications.** From a second account, perform each of
   these actions against the test account: like, repost, reply, follow,
   mention, quote.
2. **Open the Notifications tab.** Confirm a row for each new
   activity appears at the top, ordered newest first.
3. **Badge clear.** Before opening the tab, confirm the tab icon
   shows an unread count. Open the tab. Confirm the badge clears
   within 1s (`app.bsky.notification.updateSeen` fires).
4. **Reason labels.** Confirm each row's reason label matches:
   liked / reposted / followed / mentioned / replied / quoted
   (#0063 extended these to also cover `like-via-repost`,
   `repost-via-repost`, `feedgen-like`, `starterpack-joined`,
   `subscribed-post`, `contact-match`, `verified`, `unverified`).
5. **Reason icon + colour.** Confirm icons are distinct per reason
   (heart-pink, repeat-green, person-plus-blue, etc.) and stable
   in both light and dark mode.
6. **Grouping — multi-actor.** Have three different accounts like
   the same post. Refresh. Confirm a single grouped row reads
   "Alice, Bob, and 1 other liked your post" with three overlapping
   avatars (matches RN).
7. **Tap a like / repost / reply → thread.** Tap each. Confirm a
   real ThreadView opens (not the placeholder text — see #0062 /
   #0160).
8. **Tap a follow → profile.** Tap a follow notification. Confirm
   navigation to the follower's ProfileScreen (this session's
   #0062 follow-up routing).
9. **Tap a quote → thread of the quote post.** Confirm correct
   destination.
10. **Pull to refresh.** Drag the list past the top edge. Confirm
    refresh triggers and any new notifications appear at top.
11. **Infinite scroll.** Scroll past the end. Confirm next page
    loads.
12. **Subscribed-post notifications.** If you subscribe to another
    account, then they post, confirm a `subscribed-post`
    notification appears with the "shared a new post" label (#0063)
    and tapping it opens that post's thread.
13. **Read state styling.** Unread rows have a small accent dot;
    read rows do not. Confirm both render correctly.
