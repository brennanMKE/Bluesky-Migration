## Description

Manual sign-off for the post composer — text input, mention/link/
hashtag detection, image and video attachments, alt text, drafts,
threading (multi-post), replies, and quote-post composition.

## Test sequence

1. **Open composer.** From the top-right toolbar pencil (or floating
   compose on iOS, if implemented), tap. Confirm the composer sheet
   opens with the text field focused and the character counter at
   300.
2. **Plain text.** Type a 50-char post. Confirm the counter
   decrements and the post button enables.
3. **Length warning.** Type past 300 chars. Confirm the counter goes
   negative and red, and the post button disables.
4. **Mention detection.** Type "Hello @bsky.app, ". Confirm an
   autocomplete dropdown appears for matching handles, the chosen
   handle is inserted, and the rendered facet shows the handle in
   blue.
5. **Link detection.** Paste a URL. Confirm a link card preview is
   fetched and shown below the text (#0034 — link card preview).
6. **Hashtag detection.** Type "#testing". Confirm the tag is
   highlighted in the rendered text.
7. **Image attachment.** Tap the image icon. Pick up to 4 images
   from the library. Confirm thumbnails appear in a strip with an
   "Add alt text" affordance per image.
8. **Alt text.** Tap "Add alt text" on an image. Confirm a sheet
   with a text field opens. Enter alt text, save. Confirm the
   image now shows a small "ALT" badge.
9. **Image reorder / remove.** Drag-reorder thumbnails. Tap the
   `×` to remove one. Confirm count updates.
10. **Video attachment.** Tap the video icon. Pick a video (≤60s
    per Bluesky limits). Confirm a video thumbnail with a play
    icon appears (#0034).
11. **Draft persistence.** Type a post and dismiss the composer
    sheet via swipe-down or Cancel. Confirm a draft confirmation
    dialog: "Save draft? / Discard". Save. Re-open composer.
    Confirm the draft is restored (#0034).
12. **Discard draft.** Repeat #11 but Discard. Confirm the composer
    opens empty next time (#0151 — composer should not pre-fill
    stale text without a draft).
13. **Thread / multi-post.** Tap the "+" or "Add post" button to
    add another post to the same composer. Confirm a second
    composer panel appears below, with its own character counter.
    Post the thread; confirm both posts land as a connected
    thread (#0034 — thread composer).
14. **Reply.** From a thread, tap the reply icon on the focal post.
    Confirm the composer opens with the reply-to context preview
    above the text field, prefilled with `@author`.
15. **Quote post.** From a thread, tap the repost icon → Quote.
    Confirm the composer opens with the original post embedded
    below the text field.
16. **Submit a post.** Tap Post. Confirm the sheet dismisses, a
    success toast / haptic plays, and the new post appears at the
    top of the user's profile and in followers' feeds.
